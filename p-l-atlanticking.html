Atlantic King

Branding

Goal
Atlantic King wanted a new logo to represent their brand as a wholesale seafood distributor.
Solution
I started with a few mockups based on a crown and royalty but gradually moved onto a design based on Poseidon, due to the connection with the ocean. After working through a few different variations of the design, I put a focus on the waves of the ocean and finalized the design.
