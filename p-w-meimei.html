Mei Mei Bakery

Websites

Goal

Mei Mei Bakery is a local bakery that offers foods and drinks in my neighborhood. They wanted an online presence to display their food menu and attract potential customers.

Solution

I started by looking at existing websites for local restaurants. While they all varied in design and functionality, the one staple was the presence of an online menu. I took an inventory of the store's various foods and drinks and decided on a Content Management System that would allow for simple updates in the future from the store's employees, and set up the system accordingly. I proceeded to set up a template with responsive design and modern design cues, and made changes to the design, as needed. To finish up the job, I set up the food and drinks menu, as well as a contact form for customers who may have questions regarding the foods and drinks.
