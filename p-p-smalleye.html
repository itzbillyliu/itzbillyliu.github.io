Atlantic King

Packaging

Goal
Atlantic King is a wholesale company that offers various frozen seafood products. They wanted a polybag design for one of their top sellers, the Smalleye Croaker.
Solution

I started with a focus on the packaging. I decided to go with the corner ribbons to give it a packaged gift feel. I used the white background to give the customer a feeling of purity and trustworthiness, to reflect that the products are caught in the wild, rather than raised in a closed environment. The fish cut-out in the middle is meant to be transparent for the customer to be able to see what they are buying. I went with a leaf for the "Wild Caught Organic Fish" text to reflect that the product is organic and not raised in a closed environment. Lastly, I created the graphic of the sun, the fisherman, and the lake to further show that the products were caught in the wild.
