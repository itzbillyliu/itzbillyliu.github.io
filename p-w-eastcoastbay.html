Eastcoast Bay Seafood

Websites

Goal
Eastcoast Bay Seafood is a wholesale company that offers various frozen seafood products. They wanted a solution that would allow them to display their products in an online catalog, with the ability to take orders at a future date. One major requirement was the option to convert the website to Chinese for their customer base.
Solution

I started with an eCommerce Content Management System that had the ability to set multiple translations, and set it up to disable ordering of products. I proceeded to set up a template with modern design cues, and made changes to the design, as requested by the client.
