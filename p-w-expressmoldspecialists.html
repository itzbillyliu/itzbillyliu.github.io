Express Mold Specialists
Websites
Goal

Express Mold Specialists is a service company that deals with mold remediation. They wanted to improve on their previous website and increase their online presence.

Solution

My first step was finding a way to improve on their previous website. They had used static content and lacked a user-friendly way for EMS to make changes and update their information. I backed up the original pages and information, then installed a Content Management System on the server. I proceeded to set up a template with responsive design and modern design cues, making changes to the design as requested by the client.
