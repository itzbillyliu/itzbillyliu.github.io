Sun Taste

Branding

Goal

Sun Taste is a wholesale company that offers various frozen seafood products. They wanted an eye-catching cardboard container design for one of their top sellers, the Atlantic Surf Clam.

Solution

I started with a focus on the packaging. I wanted to highlight the dish of Atlantic Surf Clam, as it was much more appealing than raw surf clam. I selected a serif typeface to add appeal to the product as a more trustworthy product.
