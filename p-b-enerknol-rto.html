EnerKnol & RTO Insider

Branding

Goal

EnerKnol was aiming to increase their presence through an advertising agreement with partner RTO Insider. The ad would be included in RTO Insider's digital publication. The ad needed to reflect their product's core features and maintain all logo designs and branding colors.

Solution

With this project, I was tasked with user research, copyrighting, and design. I started with analyzing the data from customers, demos with potentials, and trial user feedback to identify pain points and where the platform's strengths lied.

Once identifying the strength of the platform (its real-time alert system), I started with the creation of a mood board and color palette for the eventual tagline, "Our users don't have FOMO."

I selected a pale blue sublayer on top of a white background, with a pale orange call to action button. I chose the white background to match the publication on which it would be printed, as well as keeping a clean, minimal design. I chose the pale blue sublayer as a subliminal nod to the sky, to represent a peaceful, calming experience when using the platform. I decided on using orange for the call to action button based on UX research that indicated that warmer colors resulted in a greater number of conversions. I selected a paler orange color for the call to action button in relation to the pale blue sublayer, to avoid too strong a contrast from the rest of the ad.
