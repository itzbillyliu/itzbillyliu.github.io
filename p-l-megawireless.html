Mega Wireless

Branding

Goal
Mega Wireless wanted a new logo to represent their brand as a wholesale multi-device accessories distributor.
Solution
I started with the idea of Mega's diverse collection of products. They sold devices from many different services and I wanted to show the connection. I created a chain link style logo in four different colors, matching the top 4 phone service providers in the United States. I added a phone icon to try and indicate that the brand is closely related to the phone industry.
