Conch king

Branding

Goal
Conch King wanted a new logo to represent their brand as a wholesale seafood distributor. They wanted a design that drew the attention to their high status and reputation.
Solution
I naturally gravitated to the idea of the crown, due to the name of the company and their preference to point to their reputation. This brought about the crown design. I felt the ribbon design added to the idea that they were a reputable business and implemented it in the finalized design.
