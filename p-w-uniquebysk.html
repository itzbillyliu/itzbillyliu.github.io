Unique Products by S&K Gifts

Websites

Goal

Unique Products by S&K Gifts is a wholesale company that offers products from multiple manufacturers. They wanted a solution that would allow them to offer these products on an online website that would take orders from both their staff, as well as their customer base. Their requirements included the ability to set order minimums, sales rep tracking, and a user-friendly, modern design to attract customers.

Solution

I started with their requirements for the order minimum. I decided on an eCommerce Content Management System that would allow for order minimums, and set up the system accordingly. I proceeded to set up a template with responsive design and modern design cues, and made changes to the design, as requested by the client. To finish up the job, I set up the system to allow for the customer to set their sales rep for backend tracking of sales by a certain rep.
